# Sayari

## Snapshot

Sayari is a commercial benchmark for graph-based ownership, sanctions, trade, and supply-chain risk intelligence across jurisdictions.

## Strong ideas

- deep corporate network visibility
- beneficial ownership traversal across jurisdictions
- integrated risk context across ownership, sanctions, and trade
- usability for analysts who need fast entity answers

## Limits for Paraguay

- It is a commercial product, not an open stack to reproduce directly.
- Its breadth and private data sourcing exceed what Centinela can or should imitate early.

## Adopt now

- use Sayari as a maturity benchmark
- prioritize analyst answerability over abstract data elegance
- keep the model ready for cross-border ownership chains

## Defer or adapt

- broad commercial-grade data coverage expectations

## Role in Centinela

Sayari is the benchmark for where the entity and ownership layer could eventually mature.
