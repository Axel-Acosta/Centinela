# Integrity Watch: Red Flags

## Snapshot

Integrity Watch: Red Flags is a public procurement risk explorer that highlights flagged procedures while emphasizing that flags are prompts for further scrutiny, not proof of wrongdoing.

## Strong ideas

- public-facing explainable flags
- filtering by authority, sector, procedure, and flag type
- clear disclaimer language about risk versus proof
- strong usability for journalists and civil society

## Limits for Paraguay

- TED-based EU data structures differ from DNCP's OCDS implementation.
- The current tool is mostly a public explorer, not a deeper entity-intelligence system.

## Adopt now

- disclaimer model
- explainable per-flag reasoning
- public-friendly flag explorer as a later direction

## Defer or adapt

- EU-specific notice structure
- dependence on TED-oriented classifications

## Role in Centinela

This is the clearest public-UX reference for a later Paraguay-facing red-flag interface.
