# QuiénEsQuién.wiki and TodosLosContratos

## Snapshot

QuiénEsQuién.wiki links contracts, companies, people, and institutions across Latin America. TodosLosContratos demonstrates how contract intelligence can be turned into a public-facing accountability experience.

## Strong ideas

- linking contracts with ownership and institutional actors
- civic-tech usability for journalists and researchers
- Latin American state-capture framing
- open-data and public investigation orientation

## Limits for Paraguay

- existing datasets and coverage are stronger in Mexico and regional Latin American contexts than in Paraguay-specific sourcing.
- the public storytelling layer should follow, not precede, backend rigor.

## Adopt now

- entity-network orientation around contracts
- public-accountability framing for later UX
- Latin American investigation sensibility

## Defer or adapt

- direct platform imitation before Centinela has enough linked entity data

## Role in Centinela

This pair of references helps define the eventual bridge from raw procurement analytics to a public accountability product.
