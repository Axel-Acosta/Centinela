# DOZORRO and ProZorro

## Snapshot

ProZorro is Ukraine's open procurement ecosystem. DOZORRO is the civic and analytical monitoring ecosystem built around it, including watchdog workflows, analytics modules, and community reporting.

## Strong ideas

- "everyone sees everything" transparency principle
- open APIs and ecosystem-enabled tooling
- civic monitoring loop around procurement red flags
- scorecards and benchmarking for procuring entities
- public and professional analytics as separate but connected layers

## Limits for Paraguay

- The Ukrainian civic ecosystem and complaint structures do not map one-to-one to Paraguay.
- Procurement-specific community workflows need local institutional and civil-society adaptation.

## Adopt now

- procurement data as ecosystem infrastructure, not a single app
- risk-routing and follow-up mindset
- later separation between internal analyst tooling and public explanatory surfaces

## Defer or adapt

- direct replication of complaint or escalation flows
- Ukrainian-specific policy categories and institutional structures

## Role in Centinela

This reference shapes how procurement intelligence can become useful in practice: not just identifying risk, but enabling oversight, triage, and public accountability.
