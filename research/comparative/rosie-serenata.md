# Rosie / Operacao Serenata de Amor

## Snapshot

Rosie is an AI-driven suspicion engine for Brazilian public spending, paired with Jarbas as the human-facing validation interface.

## Strong ideas

- suspicion-first, not guilt-first framing
- anomaly detection as a hypothesis generator
- human validation after automated detection
- public communication that explains what is suspicious and why

## Limits for Paraguay

- the source domain is reimbursement spending, not procurement.
- isolated anomaly detection without durable entity or procurement context would be too narrow for Centinela.

## Adopt now

- strong distinction between suspicious and irregular
- pair algorithmic output with a human review surface
- keep public language cautious and accessible

## Defer or adapt

- direct reimbursement-style anomaly model

## Role in Centinela

Rosie helps shape tone, caution, and the handoff from automation to human investigation.
