# OpenSanctions, Open Ownership, OpenCorporates, and ICIJ Offshore Leaks

## Snapshot

These systems form the future enrichment layer for sanctions, PEPs, company resolution, beneficial ownership, and offshore network context.

## Strong ideas

- FollowTheMoney-style entity modeling and reconciliation
- beneficial ownership statements and provenance discipline
- company matching through identifiers and reconciliation APIs
- offshore and foreign-company linkage beyond national procurement data

## Limits for Paraguay

- Licensing, access limits, and attribution requirements differ by source.
- External enrichment should never override local source facts.

## Adopt now

- design identifiers and relationships so these sources can plug in later
- keep room for multiple competing ownership claims and provenance
- treat enrichment as layered evidence, not ground truth

## Defer or adapt

- live integration before core Paraguay procurement ingestion is stable

## Role in Centinela

This layer is essential for the jump from procurement monitoring to broader integrity intelligence.
