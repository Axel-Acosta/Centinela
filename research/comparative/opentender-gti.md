# OpenTender / Government Transparency Institute

## Snapshot

OpenTender and GTI-associated work focus on procurement analytics, integrity benchmarking, and corruption-risk indicators at contract and authority level.

## Strong ideas

- comparable contract-level risk indicators
- market and authority benchmarking
- measurement of corruption risk as an analytical proxy, not direct accusation
- strong methodology and limitation framing

## Limits for Paraguay

- GTI's corruption risk frameworks can become statistically heavy and opaque if copied too early.
- Some indicator families rely on richer historical and competitive data than the first Paraguay slice currently ingests.

## Adopt now

- benchmark mentality
- explicit methodology and limitation sections
- contract and buyer-level aggregation path

## Defer or adapt

- heavy composite indices until more Paraguay data is integrated

## Role in Centinela

OpenTender/GTI mainly shapes the medium-term analytics layer once process-level ingestion expands into historical procurement series.
