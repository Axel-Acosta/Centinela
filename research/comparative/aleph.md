# OCCRP Aleph

## Snapshot

Aleph is an investigative data platform built to search structured and unstructured data, cross-reference entities across datasets, and support collaborative investigative workflows.

## Strong ideas

- entity-centric investigation workspaces
- strong search and filtering across datasets
- document ingestion and OCR-backed discoverability
- cross-reference and reconciliation workflows
- network and timeline exploration as investigation aids

## Limits for Paraguay

- Aleph is a broad investigative platform; Centinela's first need is a narrower corruption-risk and procurement-intelligence backbone.
- Full document intelligence and access control systems would be premature in the first implementation slice.

## Adopt now

- investigator workflow orientation
- entity reconciliation as a first-class capability
- preservation of documents and provenance for later search
- design toward search plus network exploration, not dashboards alone

## Defer or adapt

- full document vault and OCR pipeline
- multi-user workspace management

## Role in Centinela

Aleph is the main reference for later search and investigation UX. It keeps Centinela from collapsing into a pure dashboard without investigative depth.
