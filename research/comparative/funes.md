# FUNES

## Snapshot

FUNES is OjoPublico's algorithmic procurement-risk initiative for identifying risky contracts in Peru, with a Latin American investigative orientation.

## Strong ideas

- regional relevance for procurement corruption-risk work
- methodology that combines data analysis with journalistic scrutiny
- explicit attention to risky contracts rather than accusatory framing

## Limits for Paraguay

- Peru-specific procurement fields and institutional context do not transfer directly.
- Public reporting logic should not substitute for durable system design.

## Adopt now

- Latin American context sensitivity
- relationship between algorithmic suspicion and human review
- public-communication caution

## Defer or adapt

- any Peru-specific thresholds or data assumptions

## Role in Centinela

FUNES is a regional proof that risk scoring and journalistic accountability can work together without collapsing into simplistic accusations.
