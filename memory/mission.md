# Mission

Centinela exists to build a reusable integrity-intelligence capability for corruption-risk and public-integrity analysis, starting with Paraguay and later extending to other countries.

## Mission shape

- collect and organize project context
- collect and normalize public-integrity data
- link people, companies, institutions, contracts, and related records
- surface explainable risk signals and investigation leads
- preserve institutional memory across Codex runs
- support eventual analyst and public interfaces

## Operating constitution

- The active constitution for future runs lives in `memory/constitution.md`.
- Comparative references are not decorative background. Each major precedent must shape real Centinela components in the area where it is strongest.
- The project should deepen the current Paraguay/DNCP/PostgreSQL foundation while widening the system through stronger precedent synthesis.

## Guardrails

- Never present outputs as proof of guilt.
- Prefer risk signals, anomalies, and leads for review.
- Preserve provenance and explainability.
- Design for modularity and future productization.
- Treat local institutional work in Paraguay as an input, not an obstacle.
