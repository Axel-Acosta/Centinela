# Centinela Product Context

register: product

## Product Purpose

Centinela is a Paraguay public-integrity intelligence system. It helps analysts review public records, procurement relationships, source-backed identity context, candidate matches, evidence packets, and limitations without turning leads into accusations.

The current product surface is the local Command Center at `http://127.0.0.1:8787/`. It is an internal review tool, not a public launch site.

## Users

Primary users are analysts, researchers, civic-tech builders, reviewers, and project operators who need to understand a source-backed evidence trail without memorizing CLI commands.

They need a calm path from entity dossier to case packet to source artifacts, plus clear boundaries around what Centinela can and cannot conclude.

## Product Tone

Serious, grounded, careful, and precise. The interface should feel like a public-records workbench with institutional memory, not a flashy risk-score product.

## Strategic Principles

1. Dossier before dashboard.
2. Evidence trail over spectacle.
3. Review-first language everywhere.
4. Progressive disclosure before raw payloads.
5. Live data only, no fake proof.
6. Provenance and limitations stay visible.
7. Product polish must clarify trust and workflow.

## Anti-References

Avoid accusation-engine language, ornamental metrics, generic SaaS hero layouts, noisy gradients, glassy dashboards, repetitive card grids, and any UI that implies risk signals are legal conclusions.

## Canonical Proof Path

Use `CONSULTORA GUARANI SA INGENIEROS CIVILES` as the guided demonstration trail because it currently shows local identity context, accepted external identity context, source-backed case `20`, generated artifacts, source-document matches, hash checks, and publication-gate limits.

This path is still internal review context, not a public finding.
